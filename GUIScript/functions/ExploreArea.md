---
title: ExploreArea
module: GemRB
---

**Prototype:** GemRB.ExploreArea ([explored=True])

**Description:** Explores or unexplores the whole area.

**Parameters:**
  * explored:
    * False - undo explore
    * True - explore
    * all other values give meaningless results

**Return value:** N/A

**See also:** [MoveToArea](MoveToArea.md), [RevealArea](RevealArea.md)
